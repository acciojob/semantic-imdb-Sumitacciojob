Summary

The Shawshank Redemption follows Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for a crime he did not commit. Over the years, Andy forms a deep friendship with fellow inmate Ellis “Red” Redding and finds ways to maintain hope, dignity, and freedom within the prison walls.

Actors

  • Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne
  • Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding
  • Bob Gunton as Warden Norton
